Worth, Quantification, and Limits

Conventions, tradeoffs, and what the system can and cannot see

An accounting system compresses reality into numbers. But not everything can be quantified, and not every quantification is neutral. This module examines what accounting can measure and the deliberate choices embedded in how it measures them.
